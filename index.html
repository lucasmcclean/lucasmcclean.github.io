"All programming requires is a creative mind and the ability to organize your thoughts. If you can visualize a system, you can probably implement it in a computer program."


- John Ousterhout, A Philosophy of Software Design


In one of my favorite books on software engineering, Ousterhout captures what lies at the heart of my fascination with programming: software is about as close to the mind as a physical design can be. I strive every day to learn something new, create something useful, and meaningfully contribute to my community—software is one of the best tools for doing so.
